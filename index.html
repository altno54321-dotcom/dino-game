⚠️ Camera Usage Disclaimer

This game features an optional AI "Eye Tracking" mode that requires access to your webcam.

All facial recognition is processed 100% locally on your device. No video data is ever saved, recorded, sent, or uploaded anywhere.

🦖 Dino Runner

Choose how you want to play!

Keyboard: Space or Up Arrow to Jump, Down Arrow to Duck.
Mobile/Touch: Tap the TOP HALF of the screen to Jump, BOTTOM HALF to Duck.
Face Tracking: Blink (both eyes) to Jump, Wink (hold one eye closed) to Duck.
